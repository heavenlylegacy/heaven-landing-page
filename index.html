Every life is a story worth preserving.

Leave a beautiful and compelling legacy — for you or a loved one — a life story full of insight that may inspire, educate, or comfort generations to come.

Why we’re building this

Our lives are full of stories, wisdom, humor, and small moments that never make it into a formal obituary. Heaven.org is a quiet, safe place to gather those moments and keep them alive — for the people who matter most, and for future generations who may one day wander the shelves.

What Heaven.org lets you do

  • Create a private or shareable life library in your own words
  • Record stories, messages, and memories for family
  • Choose privacy, openness, or something in between
  • Let future generations “sit at the table” with you

Join the early circle

Heaven.org is just beginning. If you’d like to follow along, help shape what this becomes, or reserve a place for a story that matters to you, leave your email below.

No spam. No selling your address. Just thoughtful updates.

A shared universe of human stories

Some stories will remain private, held closely by family. Others may be opened to the world — allowing strangers to wander, listen, and learn. Heaven.org exists to hold those stories with care, dignity, and respect.
