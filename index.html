Every life is a story worth preserving.

Leave a beautiful and compelling legacy — for you or a loved one — a life story full of insight that may inspire, educate, or entertain generations to come.

Why we’re building this

Our lives are full of stories, wisdom, humor, and small moments that never make it into a formal obituary. Heaven.org is a quiet, safe place to gather those moments and keep them alive for the people who matter most — or for future generations who may one day draw inspiration from your journey.

What Heaven.org will let you do

  • Create a private or shareable “life library” in your own words.
  • Record stories, messages, and memories for your family.
  • Choose whether your story is just for loved ones, or part of a larger universe of inspiring lives.
  • Let future generations “sit at the table” with you – like in the image above.

Join the early circle

No spam. No sharing your address. Just occasional updates as Heaven.org takes shape.

A shared universe of human stories

Some people will keep their Heaven.org space private – just for family. Others may choose to open their story to the world, so strangers can wander the shelves and discover lives that move them, teach them, or simply make them smile. Our job is to hold those stories with care, integrity, and respect.
